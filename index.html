Connect Your Facebook Page

To set up Facebook publishing, we need:

  1. Your Facebook Business Page URL (not your personal profile)
  2. The email address of the Facebook account that manages this page
  3. Confirmation that you have admin access to the page
Important: You must be an Admin or Editor of the Facebook page to complete this connection. If you're not sure, check your page roles in Facebook Business Manager.
Important: This must be the email address of the Facebook account that has admin access to your business page. We'll use this email to send you a secure OAuth link to complete the Facebook connection.

How the connection process works:

  1. Submit this form with your Facebook page details
  2. Our team adds you to the beta tester list
  3. You receive an email with a secure OAuth link
  4. Click the link to authorize with Facebook
  5. We automatically exchange for a long-lived token
  6. We verify your connection works
  7. You receive confirmation when everything is ready

Note: This process typically takes 1-2 business days. You'll receive email updates at each step.
